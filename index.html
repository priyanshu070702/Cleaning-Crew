Why clean surrounding is important

The role of every individual in keeping the surrounding clean

  • Cleanliness is not a necessity, but a way of life. Both personal hygiene and clean surroundings promote healthy living. Being aware of how to maintain cleanliness is very important. Just like a child takes its first steps and learns to walk.
  • Cleanliness is very important for us. It is our duty to keep our surroundings clean. If we clean our surroundings we can stand as an example to others.
  • Individual society plays a significant role in protecting our surroundings by creating awareness and educating people about the need to conserve and manage natural resources.
  • Don’t litter:  Littering in public places is to be avoided. It is a very good habit to use dustbins, because a small litter can turn into garbage over time leading to bad health effects and pollution.
  • Plant trees:Planting tree is a very good and simple deed to protect our environment. It takes carbon dioxide present and releases oxygen. Due to rapid industrialization there is cutting down of trees. This can be compensated by planting trees in the surrounding.

Why clean surrounding is important?

  • A clean surrounding is essential for healthy living: The more you don’t care about our environment, the more it will become polluted with contaminants and toxins that have a harmful impact on our health.
  • Earth Is Warming: For the sake of our children and our future, we must do more to combat climate change. Yes, it’s true that no single event makes a trend. Now it not possible to ignore that.
  • Because of pollution, our environment gets dirty and so, non renewable energy sources going to depletion and will extinct at a time so requirement and role of non-renewable energy sources becomes very vital in future.
  • Cleanliness is next to godliness. We need a clean surrounding.
  • A clean environment is favorable to healthy living, learning, work and recreation.

SOCIETAL CLEANLINESS

WHAT IS SOCIETAL CLEANLINESS?

  • Social cleanliness is a mechanism in which a society or a group of people take an initiative to clean their society/surrounding which includes disinfecting the area using sterilization that prevents spread of harmful disease causing microorganisms.
  • Societal cleanliness needs active participation of all the people living in a society ,as an integrated societal cleanliness finally leads to the great national effort to keep our motherland clean.

ADVANTAGES OF SOCIETAL CLEANLINESS

  • Prevents us from disease and reduces cost of maintenance
  • Keeps us away from germs and prevents breeding of mosquitoes
  • Has psychological effect on a person (person would love to stay in a society free dirt) Cleanliness keeps individual and society in a happy mode
